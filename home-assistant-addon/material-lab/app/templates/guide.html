HOW THE GUIDE THINKS

Weighted trade-offs, not marketing.

  • Outdoor weighs UV, water and heat.
  • Stiff weighs XY rigidity and strength, but shows the impact trade-off.
  • Impact weighs impact and layer adhesion—fibre-filled composites naturally tend to lose points here.
  • High heat weighs heat score plus HDT.
  • Low cost favours lower price-range scores and printability.
The answer is a shortlist. Confirm the selected exact spool’s TDS, actual dryness and printed load direction before designing around it.
